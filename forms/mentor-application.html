Why Become a Mentor?

  • Make a positive impact in a young girl's life
  • Develop your leadership and coaching skills
  • Share your knowledge and experiences
  • Expand your professional network
  • Gain fresh perspectives and insights

Mentor Requirements

  • At least 3 years of professional experience in your field
  • Ability to commit 1-2 hours per week for at least 6 months
  • Good communication and listening skills
  • Passion for empowering and supporting young girls
  • Pass a background check
  • Attend mentor training and orientation
