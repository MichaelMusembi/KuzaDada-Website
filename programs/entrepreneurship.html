About Our Entrepreneurship Program

Our Entrepreneurship program is designed to equip young African women with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to create and grow successful businesses. We believe in the power of economic empowerment and the potential of women-led businesses to transform communities.

Through practical, hands-on learning, mentorship from established entrepreneurs, and access to resources and networks, we prepare participants to overcome the unique challenges faced by women entrepreneurs in Africa.

Program Features

What makes our entrepreneurship program unique

Mentorship Matching

One-on-one guidance from established female entrepreneurs who understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the African context.

Pitch Practice

Regular opportunities to practice and refine your business pitch with constructive feedback from peers and mentors.

Networking Events

Connect with potential investors, partners, and customers through structured networking opportunities.

Funding Opportunities

Access to information about grants, loans, and investment opportunities specific to women entrepreneurs in Africa.

Virtual Incubator

Online workspace with resources, tools, and community support for developing your business idea.

Business Competition

Annual competition with cash prizes and investment opportunities for the most promising business ideas.
